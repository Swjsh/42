Gamma — Autonomous 0DTE SPY Engine

Phase 1 · Live v15.1

Gamma is a fully autonomous, signal-driven system executing 0DTE SPY directional options on two isolated Alpaca paper accounts. It reads live price action via TradingView MCP, validates setups against a 10-rule playbook enforced by the engine, sizes positions from risk parameters in params.json, places bracket orders via Alpaca MCP, and journals every tick decision in real time — without human intervention. A 6-agent Swarm generates pre-market hypothesis context daily at 06:00 ET.
