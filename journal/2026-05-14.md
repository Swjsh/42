### 6. Lesson of the day

After a large gap-up on a high-impact CPI print (1.5%+ gap with ribbon BULL stack pre-RTH), the PMH is not resistance — it is a reference marker. The catalyst has already been digested premarket; RTH is the extension, not the reversal. The correct model for these days is: first 30–60 minutes IS the breakout, and arming a "rejection at PMH" prediction creates a false mental obstacle. Tomorrow's premarket should NOT default to expecting the new ATH (749.37) to hold as a tight cap — instead, grade the session open's momentum before deciding if 749.37 is structural resistance or another launch ramp. Catalyst-day regime and normal-day regime require different playbook templates.
