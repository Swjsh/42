### What J got right (and we are baking into the playbook)
- **Confluence read.** Three signals stacked at the same moment = high-conviction entry. Premarket level + multi-day trendline + ribbon flip.
- **Patience.** Held through the brief adverse move at entry without flinching. The ribbon-stack told you the trade was alive.
- **Ribbon-as-trailing-stop.** Didn't exit on first pullback — let candles do the work. Exited only when the leg matured.
- **Scale-out structure.** 80/20 instead of 67/33, but the *intent* matched the rule (lock most of the win, leave a runner for the home-run move).
