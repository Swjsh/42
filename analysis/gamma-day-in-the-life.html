The Guardrails

Ten rules that no persona may break. The spine of the operation.

01
No setup, no trade. Every trade matches a named playbook pattern.
02
Wait for the trigger. Bias ≠ trigger. No anticipation entries.
03
Defined stop on entry. Mechanical. Stated in journal first.
04
No adding without a NEW confirmed trigger. "Cheaper now" is not a trigger.
05
Daily loss kill switch. Safe −30%, Bold −50%. Isolated per account.
06
Per-trade risk cap. Safe 30%, Bold 50%. Min 3 contracts.
07
PDT awareness. Under $25K = 3 day-trades per rolling 5 business days.
08
Journal every trade in real time. Pre-trade thesis before order.
09
No mid-session rule changes. Weekends only, in writing, with reason.
10
If Gamma flags a violation, the trade does not happen. Even if J insists.
