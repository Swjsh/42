### 8.1 Closed-bar entry lag at named levels (09:35–09:45 window)

**Evidence (n=1, conf=0.65 — LOW, informational):**

5/15 BEARISH_REJECTION entered at 09:46 on the BOUNCE off 738.10 PML, not on the wick itself. The 09:40 bar wicked to 737.96 (below 738.10) and closed at 738.64. By the time the next bar confirmed the level break, SPY had bounced $0.68 from the wick low. Entry at the bounce ($3.14) vs entry at the wick close ($3.55 estimated) = missed $0.41 of premium expansion = $410 uncaptured HWM.

**Proposed tuning:** Add a conditional live-price trigger scoped to: (a) named ★★+ levels (PML, PMH, Carry), (b) 09:35–09:45 ET session window only, (c) bar must wick through the level AND close back across it within the same bar (single-bar sweep pattern).
