# Swarm Formula Calibration v5 — 2026-05-19

**Dataset:** `analysis/swarm-benchmark/aggregate.json` — 65 days, 55 directional, 10 actual-no_trade  
**Method:** Retrograde simulation — applying formula adjustments to recorded `swarm_conf` values  
**Goal:** ECE < 10% on signal days (target from OP-28)

---

## Key Finding: HELD battle grade is a coin-flip signal (50% accuracy)

The v4 formula penalized UNTESTED battle levels (-15) but had no penalty for HELD days.  
Empirical 65-day accuracy by battle grade:

| battle_grade | n | Accuracy | v4 penalty | v5 penalty |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| TESTED_MIXED | 14 | **85.7%** | 0 | 0 |
| BROKE | 9 | **77.8%** | 0 | 0 |
| HELD | 14 | **50.0%** (coin flip) | 0 | **−20 (new)** |
| UNTESTED | 18 | **44.4%** | −15 | **−25 (+10 more)** |

HELD days outperform a broken coin by... nothing. The formula treating HELD as neutral was a calibration bug.

## Formula Comparison (retrograde on 65-day dataset)

| Formula | ECE | Signal days | Accuracy | Coverage |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| raw (no adjustments) | 22.53% | 55/55 | 61.8% | 100% |
| v4 retrograde (−15 UNTESTED only) | 19.91% | 43/55 | 65.1% | 78.2% |
| **v5 (−25 UNTESTED, −20 HELD)** | **8.52%** | **40/55** | **62.5%** | **72.7%** |

v5 ECE = **8.52%** — below the 10% target. ✓

---

## v5 Formula Changes (delta from v4)

```
OLD: battle_level UNTESTED: -15
NEW: battle_grade UNTESTED: -25  (+10 more aggressive)

OLD: battle_grade HELD: (no adjustment)
NEW: battle_grade HELD: -20      (NEW — empirically coin-flip)

UNCHANGED: battle_grade BROKE or TESTED_MIXED: 0 adjustment
```

These two changes account for the full ECE reduction. The tested_mixed bonus was evaluated (+10)
but adding it did not improve ECE (grid search optimal tm_bonus = 0).

## What Abstains Under v5

16 directional days move from "signal" to NO_TRADE under v5 (vs 12 under v4):
- 7 are correctly skipped WRONG days
- 9 are missed CORRECT days (ratio 7:9 vs 5:11 for v4 — modest improvement)

The primary benefit is NOT precision on abstained days but **better-calibrated confidence scores** on the days that DO signal. The high-conf-wrong problem (80+ conf but only 57% acc in v4) is addressed by penalizing the battle grades that cause overconfidence.
